FAQ

Currently Catch is opertaing in California, United States. We hope to grow into other states and countires as long as there is demand. We would love for you to share your experience about this amazing service to your family and friends.

You can request a ride through the main homepage. Requesting a ride requires an account which you can make at the top right of your screen.

 

Here at Catch we pride oursleves in ensuring that Drivers and Riders feel safe whenever using the platform. There are key features we have to report any misconduct. Any driver or rider will have the ability to cancel their ride. We also plan to introduce a rating system to ensure riders and drivers are kept to a good standard. You can also read some of our safety policies here.

Crrently Catch is only available in english. We hope to add more languages and accesibility options as our platform grows and expands.

 

To register as a driver you must first make an account. You must provide a screenshot driver license and vehicle information. Once you register as driver, we will run a background check. This will take up to 3 business days.

All forms of transaction are online. We currently accept Visa,Master and Discover. We plan to intergrate Dogecoin becuase it is going to the moon.
