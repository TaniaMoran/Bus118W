Safety

Here at Catch, our number 1 priority is safety. We make sure all of the individuals who interact with our platform treat each other with the upmost respect and have methods to reach out regarding any concerns.

For Riders

Once a ride has been confirmed, riders can see who their driver is, their rating and their number plate. All of our drivers go through a background check before getting approved on our platform.

For Drivers

Our drivers are as equal as our riders. We have measures in place where rider can be rated by their drivers to promote good behavior. Drivers also have options of reprting any issues with a dedicated line.

Coronavirus

The pandemic has affected people in many ways and we make sure that we take the best precautions that is advised by the CDC. We mandate wearing masks and limit exchanges in order to prevent potential spread.
