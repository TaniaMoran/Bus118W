How our rewards work

You earn points from driving during busy driving hours. Use the Catch driver app to help find when the busiest hours are in your area. Keeping your driver score high will also help you maintain different teirs. Score above a 50% to have access to tier 3 rewards, score above a 70% to access tier 2 rewards, and score above a 90% to access tier 1 rewards. Depending on your tier, you can unlock rewards such as cashback on groceries, dining, travel, and gas. You can also receive free roadside assistance.
